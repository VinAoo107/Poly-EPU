How Market Contribution is Calculated

Entropy

Information entropy measures uncertainty in market outcomes. When price ≈ 0.5, entropy is maximum (highest uncertainty).

Entropy = -P×log₂(P) - (1-P)×log₂(1-P)

Volatility

Price volatility calculated using EWMA (Exponentially Weighted Moving Average) with decay factor λ = 0.94.

Vol_t = λ×Vol_{t-1} + (1-λ)×|ΔP_t|

Market Score

Combined measure of static (entropy) and dynamic (volatility) uncertainty with equal weights (α = 0.5).

Score = α×Entropy + (1-α)×Volatility

Contribution

Market's contribution to daily EPU is its score weighted by trading volume proportion.

Contribution = Score × (Volume / TotalVolume)
